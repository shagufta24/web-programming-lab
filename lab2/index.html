ABOUT US

Craving some delicious Italian food? Maybe you're in the mood for some spicy Biryani? No matter what kind of meal you have in mind, Belle Vie Cafe is ready to prepare it for you. Since 2004, we have been the go-to diner for the youth of Hyderabad. The first of its kind "gourmet diner" focusing on the Indian youth, we aim to bring to you a fusion of Indian cuisine with foods from around the world.

Our motto of "Every dish has a story to tell" comes to life with our unique, modernist approach to food, where our guests not only enjoy culinary innovations, but also the theatrical presentation of our dishes. We're here to serve you the best gourmet diner experience of your life. Book a table now!
